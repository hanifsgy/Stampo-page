The experience

◐

Lives in the Dynamic Island

The camera expands from the Dynamic Island when you tap to capture — a UX no other camera app offers.

▭

Tactile print animation

Photos extrude from the print slot with a slow, tactile printing animation. Worth the wait, every time.

✦

Draggable, rotatable stamps

Each capture lands on your dot-grid canvas. Pinch, pan, rotate — your moments, your layout.

⤢

Fluid zoom transitions

Tap any stamp to revisit the full memory with a matched-transition zoom. Flip it to see the postmark date.

◇

Liquid Glass controls

Native iOS 26 Liquid Glass toolbar and buttery 120fps interactions on iPhone 17 Pro.

∞

Endless canvas

Pin a great Tuesday lunch next to last summer's beach trip. Cluster a road trip into a corner. Sort by mood, not by date.
